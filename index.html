About LOKIDORMOI

Ochan LOKIDORMOI is a Passionate software engineering student at the African Leadership University in Rwanda and ALX africa, driven by a deep belief in the transformative power of education. Born and raised in South Sudan, I draw inspiration from the hard work and dedication of my parents and grandparents, who worked tirelessly to create a better life for our family. Committed to making a positive impact, I actively engage with my church and community, advocating for the importance of education, particularly among young people. My career goal is to leverage my software development skills to improve government services in South Sudan, creating user-friendly, secure, and accessible online platforms that enhance transparency and efficiency. Eager to expand my knowledge and network, I'm dedicated to making the most of my time at ALU and meeting like-minded individuals who share my passion for education and community betterment.

My Projects

TOYALI

Tikkun Olam, meaning “ to repair the world,“ is a new initiative at African Leadership University (ALU) aimed at inspiring and empowering students to give back to their community. We believe in the importance of making a positive impact and strive to instill the values of kindness, generosity, and responsibility in our students. Join us on this journey to create a better world, one act of kindness at a time.


OUR MISSION

Our mission is to inspire young people to participate in various forms of actions to make the world a better place and instill the importance of giving back to the community to create a more equitable future for all.


OUR VISION

To empower and inspire a generation of conscious and responsible global citizens who take action to create a more just and equitable world.
